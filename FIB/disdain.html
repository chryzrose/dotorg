Having determined through the process of discrimination that the response to one's dissatisfactory state is - well, dissatisfactory - this stage represents the utter contempt for such a failure. Noticeable behaviors exhibited in this stage may include twitching tails, looks of death, and offering views of one's back side. This stage nearly always results in a return to the stage of DISSATISFACTION. In fact, the two stages have been noted by many researchers to be almost indistinguishable. However, disdain can only occur after having proceeded through the previous stages.
